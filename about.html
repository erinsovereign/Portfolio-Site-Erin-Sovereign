About

My Story

I’m Erin. I got my Bachelor’s degree from TCU (go frogs!) in Marketing. But I discovered early on that I wanted to go beyond marketing. I have always liked the idea of getting people excited about something and I’ve always loved technology. After four years of working as high-level support in the technology and software industry, I recognized that I could combine the two. And thus, my desire to become a UX Designer was born.
I took an intensive ten month UX Design Course through CareerFoundry. It takes you through life as a UX Designer, while having you design a web application of your own from research all the way to a high-fidelity prototype.
